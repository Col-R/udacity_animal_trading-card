Armadillo

Armadillos have bad eyesight, but a great sense of smell. They use their noses to hunt for food.

  • Scientific Name: Dasypus
  • Life Span: 12-15 years
  • Diet: Insects and Worms
  • Habitat: Central America, South America, Texas

There are 21 different kinds of armadillo. All but one of them live in the hot and humid parts of Central and South America.
The 9-banded armadillo is the only species that lives in America, and is commonly found in Texas.
